01 What insixlive does — and what it doesn't

What we build

insixlive generates complete, professional websites for businesses, freelancers, and local services. Our AI writes all the copy, builds all the pages, and deploys a production-ready Next.js website directly into your own Vercel account — in about six minutes.

We build websites for:

  • Local service businesses — plumbers, electricians, cleaners, builders, mechanics
  • Health & beauty — dental clinics, hair salons, physiotherapy, spas, gyms
  • Food & hospitality — cafés, restaurants, bakeries, catering
  • Professional services — lawyers, accountants, consultants, architects, agencies
  • Creative & freelance — photographers, designers, coaches, educators
  • Informational & community — NGOs, schools, churches, events
  • Portfolio sites — showcase work, attract clients, present yourself

What we do not build — and why

⚠ We do not build online stores (e-commerce)

insixlive cannot build a website where customers browse products and pay you directly through the site.

Why e-commerce is not possible

An online store needs to receive payments on your behalf — money that customers pay goes through the website and must reach you, the business owner. This requires:

  • A Stripe or payment processor account linked specifically to your business
  • Legal and banking verification (KYC — Know Your Customer) that Stripe performs with you directly
  • Payout settings — your bank account details connected to Stripe so funds transfer to you
  • Tax configuration — VAT rates, regional tax rules applied per product/country
  • Order management — a backend database of orders, inventory, refunds, shipping

None of this can be set up on your behalf by insixlive. We do not have access to your banking information, and we cannot legally accept payments that belong to your business. Every online store needs to be configured individually by the business owner.

ℹ If you sell products

If you sell physical or digital products, we can build you a catalogue website that showcases your products and tells customers how to buy (phone, email, WhatsApp, in-store). The website drives interest and contact — the actual transaction happens through your existing process.

Alternatively, we can include a "Buy" button that links to an external platform you already use (Etsy, Gumroad, Shopify, etc.).

03 Your Vercel account explained

What is Vercel?

Vercel (vercel.com) is a cloud hosting platform. It is where your website lives — the servers that store your website files and make them available on the internet at all times.

Vercel has a completely free Hobby plan that is more than sufficient for the vast majority of small business websites. You will not be charged by Vercel simply for having your website hosted there.

Why does insixlive deploy to YOUR Vercel, not ours?

Most website builders host your website on their own servers — which means if you leave, your website disappears. insixlive works differently:

  • Your website is deployed to your own Vercel account, which you control
  • If you stop using insixlive, your website keeps running — nothing changes
  • You can add collaborators, edit the code, and manage everything from your Vercel dashboard
  • You are never dependent on insixlive staying in business
ℹ Creating your free Vercel account

Go to vercel.com and click "Sign Up". You can sign up with your email, GitHub, Google, or GitLab account. The free Hobby plan requires no credit card.

Once created, return to insixlive and connect your account using the "Connect Vercel" button in your dashboard.

What does the Vercel connection allow insixlive to do?

When you authorize insixlive to access your Vercel account, we are granted permission to:

  • Create new projects — one project per website you generate
  • Deploy code — push the generated website files to that project
  • Read project information — to show you your websites in your dashboard
⚠ What insixlive cannot do

We cannot access your other Vercel projects, delete anything, change your billing settings, or access your payment information. Our access is limited to the projects we create for you.

How to revoke access

If you ever want to disconnect insixlive from your Vercel account:

  1. Log in to vercel.com
  2. Go to Account Settings → Integrations
  3. Find "insixlive" and click Remove

Your websites will continue to run normally — removing access only prevents insixlive from deploying new websites in the future.

04 Your generated site URL — who can see it?

The .vercel.app address

When your website is generated, it immediately gets a public URL that looks like this:

https://your-business-name-abc123.vercel.app
✓ This URL is public — everyone can access it

Your .vercel.app address is publicly accessible to anyone in the world, right away, with no extra steps. You can share it on WhatsApp, email it to customers, post it on Instagram, or add it to a business card.

There is no "private mode" — the moment your site is deployed, it is live.

So why would I need a custom domain?

You don't need one — your website works perfectly without it. However, a custom domain has significant advantages:

  • Professionalism — mybusiness.ro looks far more credible than mybusiness-abc123.vercel.app
  • Memorability — customers can type your address directly into a browser
  • Trust — clients, partners, and Google take a proper domain more seriously
  • Email — a custom domain lets you use a matching email address like contact@mybusiness.ro
  • SEO — search engines rank websites with custom domains higher over time

The vercel.app URL will always work

Even after you add a custom domain, your original .vercel.app address continues to work. Both addresses point to the same website. You can give out either one.

05 Getting a custom domain

What is a domain name?

A domain name is the address people type to find your website — for example mybusiness.ro or clinicadentar.com. You rent a domain name from a registrar (a company that manages domain registration) and pay annually — typically between €8 and €30 per year depending on the extension (.com, .ro, .eu, etc.).

Option A — Buy a domain through Vercel (simplest)

Vercel lets you search for and buy a domain directly from your dashboard. The domain is immediately connected to your website with no DNS configuration needed.

1
Log in to vercel.com Go to your Vercel dashboard and open the project for your insixlive website.
2
Open Domains settings In your project, click Settings (top navigation) → Domains.
3
Search for a domain Type the domain name you want (e.g. mybusiness.ro) in the search field. Vercel will show availability and pricing.
4
Purchase Enter your payment details and complete the purchase. The domain is automatically connected to your project — no further setup needed.

Option B — Buy from another registrar, then connect

You can buy a domain from any registrar — popular choices include Namecheap, GoDaddy, Porkbun, Cloudflare, ROTLD.ro (for .ro domains), or GoDaddy.ro. Prices are similar everywhere; Porkbun and Namecheap tend to be cheapest.

ℹ .ro domains

If you want a .ro domain (Romanian), you can register it at rotld.ro, GoDaddy.ro, or most international registrars. A .ro domain costs approximately €10–20/year.

Section 05 — Getting a custom domain
06 Connecting your domain to Vercel (DNS setup)

If you bought a domain from a registrar other than Vercel, you need to point it to your website by updating your DNS records. This sounds technical but takes only a few minutes once you know what to do.

What is DNS?

DNS (Domain Name System) is like a phonebook for the internet. When someone types mybusiness.ro into their browser, DNS tells the browser which server to go to. By updating your DNS records, you tell the internet "this domain should go to my Vercel website."

Step 1 — Add the domain in Vercel

1
Open your Vercel project Log in to vercel.com → click your project → click Settings → click Domains.
2
Add your domain Type your domain name (e.g. mybusiness.ro) and click Add. Also add www.mybusiness.ro if you want the www version to work too.
3
Vercel shows you the DNS records to add After adding the domain, Vercel displays the exact DNS records you need. They look like the table below. Keep this page open — you will need these values in Step 2.

The DNS records Vercel will give you

Vercel provides two types of records. You need to copy these into your registrar's DNS settings:

TypeName / HostValue / Points toPurpose
A @ (or blank) 76.76.21.21 The root domain (mybusiness.ro)
CNAME www cname.vercel-dns.com The www version (www.mybusiness.ro)
ℹ Always use the values Vercel shows you

The values above are the standard Vercel DNS records, but always copy the exact values from your Vercel dashboard — they are occasionally updated.

Step 2 — Add the records at your registrar

Log in to wherever you bought your domain (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Porkbun, ROTLD, etc.) and find the DNS settings or DNS Management section for your domain. The exact location varies by registrar but it is usually under "Manage Domain" or "Advanced DNS."

1
Find DNS Management In your registrar's dashboard, find your domain and look for "DNS", "DNS Records", "Manage DNS", or "Name Servers."
2
Add the A record Click "Add Record." Select type A. Set the host/name to @ and the value to 76.76.21.21. Save.
3
Add the CNAME record Add another record. Select type CNAME. Set the host/name to www and the value to cname.vercel-dns.com. Save.
4
Wait for DNS propagation DNS changes can take anywhere from 5 minutes to 24 hours to spread across the internet. Most registrars update within 15–30 minutes. Once done, your domain will show a green checkmark in your Vercel dashboard and your website will load at your custom address.
✓ SSL is automatic

Once your DNS is connected, Vercel automatically issues a free SSL certificate (HTTPS). Your website will be secure — you do not need to do anything for this.

07 Buying a domain directly on Vercel

Buying a domain through Vercel is the easiest option because the domain is connected to your website automatically — no DNS configuration needed.

1
Go to your Vercel project Log in at vercel.com. Click on the project insixlive created for you (it will be named after your business).
2
Open Settings → Domains Click the Settings tab at the top of your project page, then click Domains in the left sidebar.
3
Search for a domain Type the name you want in the search box (for example: mybusiness.ro). Vercel will show whether it is available and the annual price. Try different extensions if your preferred name is taken.
4
Click "Purchase Domain" Add a payment method (credit or debit card) and confirm. The domain is registered in your name and immediately assigned to your project.
5
Done — no DNS setup needed When you buy through Vercel, DNS is automatically configured. Your website will be accessible at your new domain within a few minutes.
ℹ Domain renewal

Domains are renewed annually. Vercel will charge your saved payment method automatically when renewal is due — usually 30 days before expiry. Make sure your payment details stay up to date to avoid your domain expiring.

What if the domain name I want is taken?

Popular one-word domains are almost always registered. Here are strategies to find a good alternative:

  • Try a different extension — if mybusiness.com is taken, try mybusiness.ro or mybusiness.eu
  • Add your city or country — dentistcluj.ro, coafura-bucuresti.ro
  • Add a descriptor — clinicadentar-popescu.ro
  • Use hyphens — my-business.ro (avoid more than one hyphen)
  • Shorten it — dentpro.ro instead of clinicadentistapopescu.ro
⚠ Avoid very long domains

Keep your domain under 15–20 characters if possible. People need to be able to remember and type it without errors.

08 Frequently asked questions
Do I need to know anything about technology or coding?
No. insixlive is designed for business owners with no technical knowledge. You describe your business in plain language and we handle everything else. The form is simple and takes about 5 minutes to fill in.
How long until my website is live?
From submitting your form to having a live website takes approximately 6 minutes. The AI generation and deployment happen automatically while you wait.
Is there a monthly fee?
No. insixlive charges €59.99 once. That's it. Vercel hosting is free on their Hobby plan for most small websites. If you add a custom domain, you pay the registrar annually (€10–30/year) — but that's separate from insixlive.
What if I don't like the result?
You can regenerate your website at any time for €59.99. Update your brief, change your style preferences, and generate a completely new version. Each generation is a fresh AI-built website based on your updated information.
Can I edit the website after it's generated?
Yes. You own the code — you can edit it directly in the Vercel interface or download it and edit with any code editor. If you're not technical, the easiest approach is to regenerate with an updated brief. You can also hire any web developer to modify the code since it's standard Next.js.
Will my website appear on Google?
Yes, but it takes time. Google needs to discover and index your website — this typically takes 1–4 weeks after launch. You can speed this up by submitting your URL to Google Search Console (search.google.com/search-console) and requesting indexing. A custom domain significantly helps your Google ranking over time.
Can I have multiple websites?
Yes. Each website generation is a separate €59.99 payment. Each generates a new, separate project in your Vercel account with its own URL.
Is the website mobile-friendly?
Yes. Every website insixlive generates is fully responsive — it automatically adapts to look correct on mobile phones, tablets, and desktop computers.
Does my website have HTTPS / is it secure?
Yes. Vercel provides a free SSL certificate automatically. Your website will always use HTTPS — the padlock icon appears in every browser. No action is needed from you.
Can I use a domain I already own?
Yes. If you already have a domain registered anywhere, you can connect it to your Vercel project by updating two DNS records as described in Section 6 of this guide. The process takes about 5 minutes plus up to 24 hours for DNS to propagate.
How much does a domain cost?
Typically between €8 and €30 per year depending on the extension. Common extensions: .com (~€12–15/yr), .ro (~€10–18/yr), .eu (~€8–12/yr). Domain cost is paid to the domain registrar (or Vercel), not to insixlive.
What happens if I close my Vercel account?
Your website would go offline. Before closing Vercel, download your website code from the project and back it up. The code is standard Next.js and can be hosted on any other platform (Netlify, Cloudflare Pages, a VPS, etc.).
Can I have a professional email like contact@mybusiness.ro?
A custom domain enables professional email, but email hosting is separate from website hosting. Vercel does not provide email. You would need to set up email through a service like Google Workspace (€5–10/month), Zoho Mail (free tier available), or your domain registrar's email service. Contact us if you need guidance on this.
Can I build a website in Romanian?
Yes. Our form is available in Romanian and you can specify that the website should be generated in Romanian. All content, copy, and page text will be written in Romanian by the AI.
My DNS is set up but the domain still doesn't work — what do I do?
DNS propagation can take up to 24 hours, though most cases resolve within 30 minutes. Check the status in your Vercel dashboard under Settings → Domains — it will show a green checkmark when DNS is properly connected. If it shows an error after 24 hours, double-check that you entered the DNS records exactly as shown in Vercel, with no extra spaces or typos.
Who do I contact if I have a problem?
Email us at contact@insixlive.com. We aim to respond within 24 hours on business days.
