Common questions.

How much will it cost?

Most pieces are quoted at consultation. Hourly rates start at $200; smaller pieces are flat-priced. A $100 deposit holds your booking and is applied to the final cost.

How do I prepare for a session?

Eat a real meal beforehand. Stay hydrated. Don't drink the night before. Wear loose clothing that gives easy access to the placement.

Aftercare?

We send you home with full instructions and a second-skin patch. Two weeks of basic care, six weeks for full healing.

Touch-ups?

One free touch-up within six months of the original session, if needed.

Walk-ins?

Not generally. Flash days are the exception — one Sunday a month, posted in advance.
