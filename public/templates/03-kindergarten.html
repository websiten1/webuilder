~ Programs

Programs that grow with your child.

🐝

Half-day

Mornings only, 8–12. Perfect first taste of school for our youngest learners.

🦊

Full-day

8 to 4, with hot lunch, rest, and full programming. Our most popular option.

🌟

Extended care

Drop-off from 7:30, pickup until 6. For working families.

🎨

Arts atelier

Wednesday afternoons in our art studio with a visiting artist.

Forest mornings

Fridays at the park, rain or shine. Boots included.

Summer camp

Two-week themed sessions through July and August. Open to non-enrolled friends.
