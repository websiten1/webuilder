— Services

Representation for both sides of the table.

— 01 · Sellers

Listing services

Comprehensive listing representation — strategy, staging, photography, marketing, and negotiation through close.

  • Pricing analysis and pre-list strategy
  • Staging consult and styling
  • Editorial photography and video
  • Direct outreach to qualified buyers
  • Open-house program when it fits
— 02 · Buyers

Buyer representation

Quiet, careful work on your side of the table — including off-market searches and contingency planning.

  • Off-market and pocket listings
  • Tour planning and on-site review
  • Bid strategy
  • Inspection management
  • Closing oversight
— 03 · Investors

Investment advisory

Portfolio acquisition, repositioning, and the boring spreadsheet that decides whether the deal is a deal.

  • Cap-rate analysis
  • 1031 exchange coordination
  • Tenant-occupied properties
  • Multifamily and mixed-use

Relocation & concierge

For clients moving in, out, or across town. The list of contractors, schools, and movers worth knowing.

  • Neighborhood walk-throughs
  • School and commute briefings
  • Vendor introductions
  • Pre-move project management
