— our shop, our story

An independent bookshop, by hand.

Five floors. Eighteen sections. A café tucked behind fiction.

Replace this with your story. The day you signed the lease. The estate of books you bought sight-unseen. The first writer who agreed to read here, against every odd.

Tell people how the shop works. Why your children's section is on the ground floor. Why poetry shares a wall with essays. Why used books are mixed in with new.

Write about your café — the espresso machine, the seasonal pastry, the regulars who buy a coffee and stay for two hours. Specifics, always specifics.

And close with what you'd like a visitor to take with them when they leave. Not always a book. Sometimes a sentence.
